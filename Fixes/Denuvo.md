# Denuvo

Games protected by [Denuvo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Denuvo) are **not playable** by default.

You can still activate and play them if you get an **offline activation** from one of the Discord servers below.

This is one of the signs that a game has denuvo.

![Denuvo sign](/_images/denuvo.png)

## Activation Servers

- [Denuvo](https://discord.gg/denuvo)
- [Anti-Denuvo Sanctuary](https://discord.gg/anti-denuvo-sanctuary)

## Important

Read each server's channels and instructions carefully before asking for help.

If you do not read/look around first, **you will not be helped**.
